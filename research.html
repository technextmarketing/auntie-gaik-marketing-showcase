04 · The Key Finding

A World-Class Reputation, a Quiet Digital Voice

Auntie Gaik Lean holds one of Malaysia's most prestigious culinary honours — the country's first Peranakan Michelin star, held four years running. Yet its digital reach hasn't kept pace with its fame. Coverage notes the restaurant is "quite unknown in the social media market," with popularity driven largely by word of mouth.

The reputation is elite. The digital footprint is modest. That distance is exactly where the growth lives.
