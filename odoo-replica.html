Point of Sale + Kitchen Display

Take your orders with precision

Point of Sale and kitchen display, integrated. Servers navigate the floor plan to add orders table by table, build set-menu combos for the Nyonya tasting platters, and send every ticket straight to the kitchen — no shouting, no lost slips.

Appointments

Table booking made simple

Guests book a table online through Odoo Appointments. Reservations land directly on your customisable floor plan, which your host can edit with a simple drag-and-drop editor — no more double-booked tables on a Sunday lunch rush.

Inventory

Never ever run out of stock

Automated and manual replenishment. Set auto purchase orders for tracked ingredients like nutmeg and belacan, and use recurring tasks for the market-day items. Create, track and validate purchase orders so stock never runs dry mid-service.

Planning

Plan your services like a pro

Smart shift scheduling for front-of-house and kitchen. Assign shifts automatically or adjust manually based on who's available, manage roles by station, and keep every server and cook informed of tomorrow's line-up.
