Point of Sale · Kitchen Display

Take your orders with precision

Point of Sale and kitchen display, fully integrated

Servers take orders on a tablet at the table. Each course fires to the kitchen display the moment it's confirmed — no paper dockets, no shouting through the pass, no missed modifiers on a busy Michelin-guide weekend.

QR ordering when the queue is long

Guests can scan a QR code to browse the menu and order from their table, easing pressure on the floor during peak tourist season.

Appointments · Floor Plan

Table booking made simple

Online reservations, around the clock

Diners plan a visit to Auntie Gaik Lean's weeks ahead — often from Singapore, Hong Kong or Europe. With Odoo Appointments they book online at any hour, and every reservation lands directly on your floor plan.

Your dining room, drawn as it really is

The floor plan mirrors the shophouse dining room table by table. Reserved tables are marked, walk-ins are seated around them, and no booking is ever taken twice.

Inventory · Purchase

Never ever run out of stock

Automated and manual replenishment

Every dish sold depletes its ingredients from stock. When fresh nutmeg, gula Melaka or belacan runs low, Odoo drafts the purchase order to your regular supplier — you approve it with one tap.

Seasonal and market-day buying, handled

Recurring tasks cover wet-market runs and seasonal produce, while trackable items reorder themselves. Costs flow straight into accounting, so food-cost percentage is always current.

"Fresh nutmeg and gula Melaka? Always on hand — the reorder went out before we noticed we were low."Kitchen team
Planning · Employees

Plan your services like a pro

Smart shift scheduling

Schedule lunch and dinner services, publish shifts to the team's phones, and let staff swap within rules you set. Attendance, leave and payroll inputs live in the same system.

One record per person, from hire to payslip

Contracts, documents and attendance sit in Odoo Employees — no more folders of paper in the back office.
